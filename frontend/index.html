Project CAHTA

Project CAHTA works towards minimizing the negative effects of traditional bottom trawling by using a Constructively Amplifying Hydroacoustic Transducer Array.

What is CAHTA?

CAHTA is a revolutionary proposed method of bottom trawling. It uses hydroacoustic transducers to attract fish into the net.
